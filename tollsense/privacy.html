Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the TollSense mobile and Android Auto application ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

1. The short version

TollSense uses your device's location to detect upcoming toll plazas and alert you while you drive. All toll detection and distance calculations happen on your device. We do not run our own servers, do not sell or share your data, and do not use advertising or tracking SDKs. TollSense never transmits your location off your device.

2. Information we access

  • Precise location (foreground & background). Used to compute your distance to toll plazas and to determine when you enter or exit a tolled highway. Location is processed in real time on your device and is not retained beyond what is needed to generate an alert.
  • No account, no personal identifiers. TollSense does not ask you to sign in and does not collect your name, email, contacts, phone number, or device identifiers. (Joining the optional launch waitlist on our website is separate — see section 11.)

3. Why we need background location

Toll alerts must work while you are driving and the App is not on screen — for example when Google Maps is in the foreground or your phone is locked. For this reason the App requests background location access and runs a foreground service while monitoring is active. A persistent notification is shown whenever monitoring is running, so you always know when location is in use.

4. Data we collect and where it goes

Toll detection and distance calculations happen entirely on your device. We do not store your location on any server we control, and we never sell or share it. Toll plaza and fare data is bundled with the App; it does not include any of your personal or location information.

5. Address lookup

To display a human-readable place name (for example, the road or town you are near), the App uses the geocoding service built into your Android device. This lookup is handled by the operating system on your device; TollSense does not send your coordinates to any TollSense server or third-party service for this purpose. Because this geocoder is part of Android, its behaviour is governed by your device and operating-system provider.

6. Permissions summary

  • Location — toll detection (described above).
  • Notifications — to display toll alerts and the monitoring status.
  • Foreground service — to keep toll monitoring running while driving.

7. Data sharing and selling

We do not share or sell any data, because we do not collect any off-device data to begin with.

8. Children

TollSense is intended for drivers and is not directed at children under 13.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the App evolves. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

10. Launch waitlist (website)

If you choose to join our launch waitlist on this website, your email address is collected through a Google Form and used solely to notify you once TollSense is available on the Play Store. That form is operated by Google and subject to Google's privacy policy. We do not add you to any other list, and you can ask us to delete your address at any time at the contact email below. This is entirely optional and separate from the App.
